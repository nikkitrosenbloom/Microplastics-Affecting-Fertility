# Science Report

### This article investigates whether microplastics actually affect human fertility, separating verified research from the social media panic sparked by a 2026 Netflix documentary. It walks through what microplastics are, where they've been detected in the human body, and the difference between correlational findings and the limited causal evidence available from animal studies and human-tissue lab work. The piece closes with practical, low-effort ways to reduce everyday microplastic exposure at home.
